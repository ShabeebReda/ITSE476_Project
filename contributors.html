Our Code of Conduct

Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual bases


Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

  • Using welcoming and inclusive language: Avoid jargon where possible, and explain terms when necessary.
  • Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences: Understand that others may have different perspectives and backgrounds.
  • Gracefully accepting constructive criticism: See feedback as an opportunity for growth.
  • Focusing on what is best for the community: Prioritize the project's success and well-being.
  • Showing empathy towards other community members: Understand and share the feelings of others.

Unacceptable Behaviour

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

  • Harassment: Offensive comments, verbal or written, related to any protected characteristic.
  • Discrimination: Treating individuals unfairly based on protected characteristics.
  • Intimidation or threats: Any form of threatening or intimidating behaviour.
  • Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments: Personal attacks or demeaning remarks.
  • Public or private harassment: Unwanted attention or persistent communication.
  • Publishing others' private information: Without explicit permission.

Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behaviour and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behaviour that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
